**Retain (Safe for Production):**

What happens when PVC deleted:
1. PVC is removed
2. PV status changes to "Released"
3. **Data remains on disk intact**
4. Admin can recover or clean up manually
5. PV cannot be auto-rebound (requires manual reclaim)

**Recycle (Deprecated):**
- Runs `rm -rf` on volume
- Not recommended, use Delete or Retain

**Use Retain:**
- Production databases
- Critical application data
- Compliance requirements
- When manual review needed before deletion
