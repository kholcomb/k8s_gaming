What to Look For:
==================

Your service exists and pods are running, but you're seeing intermittent errors when 
pods restart. Sometimes requests work, sometimes they fail with connection errors.

Check These Areas:
------------------

1. **Service Endpoints**
   Check what endpoints the service has:
   ```bash
   kubectl get endpoints web-service -n k8squest
   ```

   Do you see pod IPs listed?

2. **Pod Status**
   Check pod readiness:
   ```bash
   kubectl get pods -n k8squest -l app=web
   ```

   Look at the READY column:
   â€¢ 1/1 = Ready
   â€¢ 0/1 = Not ready

3. **Pod Events**
   Check pod events:
   ```bash
   kubectl describe pod web-app-1 -n k8squest
   ```

   Look for:
   â€¢ "Readiness probe failed"
   â€¢ "Readiness probe succeeded"
   â€¢ Or... NO readiness probe messages at all?

4. **The Problem**
   If you see NO readiness probe messages, that's the issue! Without a readiness 
   probe, Kubernetes doesn't know when pods are actually ready.
