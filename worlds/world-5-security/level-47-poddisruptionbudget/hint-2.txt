## PodDisruptionBudget

Ensures minimum availability during voluntary disruptions (drains, updates).

```yaml
spec:
  minAvailable: 2  # Keep 2 pods running
  # OR
  maxUnavailable: 1  # Max 1 pod down
```

## Your Problem

- Deployment: 2 replicas
- PDB: minAvailable 3
- 3 > 2 = Impossible!

## Solutions
